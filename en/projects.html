Featured Projects

Selected projects that best represent my approach to engineering analysis: identifying the governing physics, building appropriate models, and translating results into useful technical insight.

Rotary Kiln Damper Optimization

Industrial CFD analysis of high-temperature gas-air mixing and downstream thermal control

Context: Industrial gas treatment system operating under high-temperature conditions, where passive air suction was used to modify pressure and temperature downstream.

Approach: CFD modeling of gas-air mixing, species transport, ideal gas behavior, and temperature-dependent properties.

Value: Evaluation of flow behavior and operating feasibility in a system where thermal control and physical consistency were critical.

PCM Thermal Storage Modeling

Finite element study of phase change materials in thermal energy systems

Context: Thermal modeling of phase change materials for systems where transient heat transfer and energy storage behavior are central.

Approach: Finite element formulation with nonlinear solution methods and adaptive meshing strategies.

Value: Better understanding of thermal behavior, phase change dynamics, and computational efficiency.

Hydraulic Flow and Distribution Analysis

Applied modeling of flow behavior in hydraulic and industrial systems

Context: Engineering analysis of flow distribution, discharge conditions, and hydraulic feasibility under realistic operating conditions.
