Introduction

This section gathers finite element projects developed in FreeFEM and documented with greater numerical and technical depth.

The purpose is not only to present simulation results, but also to show the structure of the model: problem definition, variational formulation, treatment of domains and interfaces, numerical implementation, validation, and interpretation of results.
